Cumulative Carbon Emissions

The countries with history of revolutions such as England and France have accumalated the most emissions over time.

Carbon Emission per capita

There are very large inequalities in per capita emissions across the world. Cocuntries that don't have huge populatitons such as Australia are producing more emissions than countries much smaller in populations as compared to them.

GDP impact on Carbon Emissions

There is generally a positive corelation between GDP and carbon emissioon that is a contry with a higher gdp is likely to have higercarbon emissions as seen with the China and USA. Population also has a positive corelation with ccarbon emissions.

Carbon Emissions by Sector

We can see that electricity and heat which can be considered as fuel contribute the most to emissions.
